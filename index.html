Matticulous Work

I started programming on my own with basic HTML and CSS. After a while my entrepreneurial side came out, and I started by own business designing sites for some businesses I was already working with. I really liked the work I was doing, and it gave me a glimpse of an entire world of technology that I knew nothing about. So, I packed up my things, moved to Oregon and enrolled at Epicodus, one of the premiere programming schools in Portland.

Despite the sometime 70 hour weeks, I loved my time at Epipcodus, and my skills as a developer grew exponentially. Primarily focusing on Angular JS, Ruby on Rails, and Design, I got to build some really cool things; from a text-based hipster adventure game to a developer networking site.

Ideally, I'd love to combine my skills as a developer with my background and education is sales/marketing and really leverage my entire skillset to help small businesses and non-profits.
